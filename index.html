Event Information & Policies

  • Setup starts 2 hours before the event, and the bar will be ready for serving by the time the event starts.
  • The maximum service time is 6 hours, and the latest end time is 2:00 a.m.
  • If there is a specific cocktail that you prefer and it is not included, please discuss it with us, and we will do our best to accommodate your request!
  • In all cases, we offer unlimited consumption. However, in case of excessive consumption of a specific cocktail, we will continue with our other options.
  • The Old Fashion Mobile Bar will have additional alcoholic beverages worth 100 euros as backup, which will be charged accordingly after the event in case of consumption (e.g., additional shots).
  • A deposit of 200 euros is required to confirm the booking/date.
  • The payment should be settled within one week after the event.
  • If you have any questions regarding the offer or any other inquiries regarding our services on the day of the event, do not hesitate to contact us to arrange a meeting or a phone call.
